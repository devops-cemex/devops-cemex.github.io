CEMEX Project Integrated LTD is a full-spectrum infrastructure company, delivering reliable energy, marine, construction, and facility management services across Nigeria. Established in 2015, we have navigated complex industry challenges through strategic leadership and an unwavering commitment to quality. Our journey is one of continuous transformation—sustained by expertise and measured by results—making us a trusted partner in building Nigeria's future.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver reliable power, marine, construction and facility management solutions that drive sustainable growth, strengthen communities and support critical operations. We are committed to excellence, innovation and efficiency, ensuring every project we undertake creates long term value for our clients and stakeholders.

Our Vision

To set a new standard of infrastructure excellence by delivering sustainable power solutions, advancing marine services, and developing iconic projects that energize communities and drive progress across Nigeria and beyond.

Team

DIRECTOR
