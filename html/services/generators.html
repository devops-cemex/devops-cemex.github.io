Industrial-Grade Power, Engineered to Last

CEMEX supplies, installs, and maintains premium diesel generators for homes, estates, industries, and large commercial operations. Every unit is commissioned by certified engineers and backed by nationwide service support, ensuring your business keeps running even when the grid does not.

We partner directly with Mikano International and authorised York distributors to deliver fully-built sound-attenuated gensets — complete with ATS, AMF control panels, and service contracts. Flexible payment plans, trade-in options, and usage-based billing are available for qualifying clients.
