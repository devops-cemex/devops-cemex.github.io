Empowering Growth Through Reliable Energy

CEMEX Power Solutions engineers and delivers reliable, efficient, and scalable energy systems that empower industries, businesses, and communities. We specialise in providing uninterrupted power customised for your precise operational needs, ensuring you can operate with total confidence and focus on sustainable growth.

From advanced renewable energy projects to comprehensive Independent Power Producer (IPP) plants, we manage the entire lifecycle. Our services eliminate downtime and financial risk by providing full asset provision, operations, and maintenance under a usage-based billing model. This zero-capital-investment approach guarantees consistent power continuity, allowing you to dedicate all your resources to your core business.

Core Capabilities

Renewable Energy Solutions

We design, build, and operate solar, gas, and diesel power plants to produce clean energy for the grid or for specific clients.

Independent Power Producer (IPP)

We operate gas, diesel, solar, and hybrid plants to deliver uninterrupted electricity for malls, estates, industries, and other large-scale clients.

Project Development

Our team expertly manages all permits, licenses, and infrastructure development required for successful renewable energy initiatives.

Net Metering

We provide on-site system installation, allowing customers to be billed for their net energy usage and optimise costs.
