More Innovative Spaces, Seamless Operations

CEMEX Facility Services provides comprehensive, integrated operations that protect and enhance the value of your property assets. We move beyond simple maintenance to deliver solutions that ensure operational safety, total efficiency, and an exceptional experience for occupants and residents.

Our expert teams manage every aspect of your building's ecosystem, from complex technical systems and energy management to landscaping, security, and hospitality. Through proactive maintenance, transparent reporting, and dedicated resident engagement, we create a responsive, high-performance environment, allowing you to focus on your strategic goals while we safeguard your investment.

Core Capabilities

Cleaning & Sanitation

Daily cleaning of common areas, waste management, pest control, and deep cleaning services.

Mechanical & Electrical Maintenance

Proactive and corrective maintenance for electrical systems, plumbing, HVAC, elevators, and fire protection systems.

Landscaping & Groundskeeping

Professional care for lawns, gardens, and outdoor common areas, including irrigation and hardscape maintenance.

Security & Access Control

24/7 staffed security, CCTV surveillance, and visitor and vehicle management systems.

Utility Management

Monitoring and management of water, electricity, backup generators, and fuel supply.

Asset Management

Maintenance of land and equipment, inventory management, and periodic tracking of all estate assets.

Resident Services & Communication

A dedicated helpdesk for resident requests, community communications, and emergency response coordination.

Administrative & Financial Support

Budgeting, financial reporting, lease management, and compliance documentation.

Health & Safety Services

Implementation of emergency preparedness drills, evacuation plans, and safety monitoring.
