Integrated Marine & Welfare Excellence

CEMEX acts as a strategic partner for the global energy sector, seamlessly integrating complex marine logistics to ensure operational flow. We unify vessel chartering, advanced fabrication, and elite staffing with rigorous safety protocols to mitigate risk and maximize efficiency. By solving the industry's toughest logistical challenges, we provide the technical certainty required to navigate even the most demanding marine environments.

A well-cared-for team is the foundation of safe and productive operations. We deploy comprehensive catering and welfare management designed to fuel workforce performance in high-demand environments. From providing nutritious, culturally diverse menus to managing full-scale crew well-being, we create a supportive atmosphere that ensures your people are healthy, motivated, and prepared to succeed.

Core Capabilities

Marine Operations

We offer flexible vessel chartering for service boats, barges, and houseboats, alongside custom equipment fabrication, drydocking, and maintenance to keep your assets safe, compliant, and efficient.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Our services include reliable leasing for essential project equipment (forklifts, tractors, portacabins), swift logistics support to streamline offshore operations, and cost-effective procurement through strong supplier partnerships.

Manpower & Workforce Solutions

We provide end-to-end recruitment, deployment, and management of skilled personnel, project managers, and supervisors tailored to the oil, gas, and energy sectors.

Catering Services

We provide round-the-clock meal preparation, including customised menu planning that accommodates special dietary, cultural, and nutritional requirements. We also cater for special events and holidays to boost crew morale.

Crew & Welfare Services

We ensure a high standard of living for offshore teams through comprehensive hospitality, housekeeping, catering, and holistic welfare programs that enhance morale and productivity.
