Clean, Silent, Predictable Power

CEMEX designs and installs turnkey solar + storage systems for homes, SMEs, and estates across Nigeria. Every bundle is sized to your actual load profile, uses Tier-1 panels and lithium batteries, and is backed by a 10-year performance guarantee.

From a 2.5 KVA starter kit that powers lights, fans, a TV, and a fridge — to estate-wide microgrids that offset diesel costs by up to 70% — we handle everything from load audit and structural survey through to NERC compliance, net-metering registration, and remote performance monitoring.

Why Go Solar With CEMEX

Tier-1 Components

Monocrystalline panels, Deye / Victron inverters, and lithium LiFePO4 batteries with 6,000+ cycle ratings.

Bespoke Sizing

We audit your actual appliances before sizing — no oversold systems, no undersized disappointments.

Spread Payments

Cash, instalment, or lease-to-own — with 12, 18, and 24-month tenors available on approved systems.

Remote Monitoring

Track generation, battery state, and fault alerts from your phone — our service team sees the same data.
