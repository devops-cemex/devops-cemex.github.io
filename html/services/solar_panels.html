Built To Last. Backed In Writing.

Solar is a long-term investment, so we put the warranty in writing. If anything covered fails, we handle the manufacturer claim for you — you don't chase parts, we do.

Solar Panels

Up to 20-year warranty on the Premium System. Panels keep producing for around 15 years across the range.

Lithium Battery

Up to 10-year warranty on the Premium System battery, rated for 6,000 charge cycles — that's many years of daily use.

Inverter

Up to 5-year warranty on the Premium System inverter. All systems use proven hybrid models that auto-switch between solar, battery, and grid.

We Handle The Claim

If something covered fails inside the warranty period, our team files the manufacturer claim and arranges the swap. You stay powered.
