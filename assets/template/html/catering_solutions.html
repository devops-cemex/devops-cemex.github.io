Hospitality That Fuels Performance

CEMEX Catering Solutions provides premier hospitality and comprehensive welfare management for crews in remote and high-demand environments. We understand that a well-cared-for team is the foundation of a safe and productive operation. Our mission is to enhance daily life for your workforce, ensuring they are motivated, healthy, and performing at their best.

We deliver more than just meals; we offer a complete hospitality experience. Our services range from meticulous meal planning—providing nutritious, balanced, and culturally diverse menus—to full-scale welfare management. Every detail is executed to the most stringent international standards of hygiene and efficiency, fostering a positive and comfortable environment that directly supports your operational goals.

Core Capabilities

Catering Services

We provide round-the-clock meal preparation, including customised menu planning that accommodates special dietary, cultural, and nutritional requirements. We also cater for special events and holidays to boost crew morale.

Hospitality & Housekeeping

Our services include maintaining clean, climate-controlled accommodations with daily housekeeping, linen changes, and complete laundry services to ensure comfort and hygiene.

Logistics & Operations

We manage the entire supply chain, from forecasting and procurement to cold chain logistics and inventory tracking, ensuring the timely delivery of all necessary supplies to offshore locations.

Crew Welfare & Support

To enhance morale and productivity, we provide recreational facilities, internet and communication services, and transportation coordination, creating a supportive and connected community offshore.
