Integrated Marine Excellence

CEMEX Marine Services delivers comprehensive offshore, inshore, and onshore support for the global energy sector. We are the strategic partner for complex marine environments, integrating vessel chartering, advanced logistics, custom fabrication, and elite staffing to ensure your operations flow without interruption.

Our focus is on mitigating risk and maximising efficiency. By unifying disparate services into one cohesive solution, we solve the industry's toughest logistical challenges. We deploy our trusted expertise and rigorous safety protocols to navigate evolving demands, providing the operational certainty you need to succeed in even the most demanding marine environments.

Core Capabilities

Marine Operations

We offer flexible vessel chartering for service boats, barges, and houseboats, alongside custom equipment fabrication, drydocking, and maintenance to keep your assets safe, compliant, and efficient.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Our services include reliable leasing for essential project equipment (forklifts, tractors, portacabins), swift logistics support to streamline offshore operations, and cost-effective procurement through strong supplier partnerships.

Manpower & Workforce Solutions

We provide end-to-end recruitment, deployment, and management of skilled personnel, project managers, and supervisors tailored to the oil, gas, and energy sectors.

Crew & Welfare Services

We ensure a high standard of living for offshore teams through comprehensive hospitality, housekeeping, catering, and holistic welfare programs that enhance morale and productivity.
