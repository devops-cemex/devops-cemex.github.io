Fully Managed Power Solutions

At CEMEX Project Power Solutions, we deliver reliable, efficient, and fully managed power solutions tailored to the precise needs of industries, businesses, and communities. Our mission is to provide an uninterrupted energy supply that empowers our clients to operate with confidence and achieve continuous growth.

From initial design and development to long-term operation, we manage every aspect of power generation and distribution. We specialize in creating consistent, scalable, and high-quality energy systems built on a foundation of reliability, efficiency, and end-to-end execution. Our Independent Power Producer (IPP) model guarantees cost-efficient energy with zero capital outlay for our clients. We assume full responsibility for asset provision, skilled operations, comprehensive maintenance, and fuel management, allowing you to focus on your core business while we ensure a dependable power supply.

Core Offerings

Our power solutions portfolio is built on a foundation of innovation, dependability, and measurable results. We deliver tailored, future-ready systems designed to ensure continuous operations, optimal efficiency, and sustainable growth for our clients.

Renewable & Hybrid Energy Systems

We design, build, and operate integrated solar, gas, diesel, and hybrid facilities engineered for maximum reliability and efficiency. Each system is optimized to reduce emissions while maintaining consistent output.

Energy Infrastructure Development

From permits to commissioning, our experts manage the entire development lifecycle of energy projects. We ensure compliance, safety, and quality—delivering ready-to-operate facilities on schedule and within scope.

Independent Power Production (IPP)

We operate Independent Power Producer plants under long-term agreements, providing guaranteed power delivery and operational excellence for industrial and commercial enterprises.

Net Metering & Smart Billing

Our advanced metering systems empower clients with transparent, data-driven insights into consumption and cost performance—maximizing control and financial predictability.

Energy Strategy & Optimization

Through detailed audits and analytical modeling, we identify efficiency gaps and create strategies that align energy management with long-term business objectives and sustainability goals.

Turnkey Power Partnerships

We provide end-to-end partnership models—from project financing and deployment to ongoing management—allowing clients to focus on core operations while we ensure seamless power continuity.

Fully-Managed Energy Solutions, Zero Capital Outlay

Move beyond strategy with our end-to-end managed power services. We leverage deep industry expertise to deliver energy systems built for peak performance and dependability. Our Core Power Offerings

Independent Power Producer (IPP)

We operate state-of-the-art Gas, Diesel, Solar, and Hybrid power plants to deliver uninterrupted, high-quality electricity to malls, residential estates, industrial parks, and other large-scale clients.

Renewable Energy Solutions
